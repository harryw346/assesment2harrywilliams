Porter Weston Robinson is an American DJ, Grammy nominated record producer and musician from Chapel Hill, North Carolina. He has released multiple number one singles across different electronic genres. His debut full-length studio album, Worlds, was released on August 12, 2014. Since then, Porter has gone on to tour all over the world, including debuting his new tour with fellow producer Madeon titled 'Shelter', a tour following the single which was released in August 2016.
